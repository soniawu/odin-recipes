Buffalo Chicken Wings



ABOUT THIS CHICKEN WINGS

Buffalo chicken wings are some of the most popular types of wings and have been for more than 50 years. It is a good item to be served in a party because everyone like it.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 quart vegetable oil for deep frying
  • 24 chicken wings, tips removed and wings cut in half at joint
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
  • 5 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste

STEPS

Step 1 - Heat the oil in a large skillet or deep fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Deep fry chicken wings in oil until done, about 10 minutes. Remove chicken from skillet or deep fryer and drain on paper towels.

Step 2 - Melt the butter in a large skillet. Stir in the, vinegar and hot pepper sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add cooked chicken to sauce and stir over low heat to coat. The longer the wings simmer in the sauce, the hotter they will be. Serve warm.
